# Product

## Identity

Life Tracker is a personal operating system.

It combines:

- planning
- finance
- learning
- career
- reflection

into one coherent experience.

---

## It is NOT

- a company ERP
- a task management clone
- a habit tracker clone
- a spreadsheet
- a KPI dashboard

---

## Emotional Tone

The product should feel:

- warm
- intentional
- editorial
- minimal
- thoughtful

Never cold.

Never corporate.

Never overwhelming.

---

## Design Inspirations

The emotional quality is inspired by:

- Apple Journal
- Day One
- Reflect
- Readwise
- Notion
- Linear

Do not copy their UI.

Understand their philosophy.

---

## Product Philosophy

Every page should answer one meaningful question.

Examples

Dashboard

"How am I doing?"

Finance

"Am I making healthy financial decisions?"

Career

"Am I becoming more valuable?"

Goals

"Am I moving toward what matters?"

Reflection

"What have I learned?"

Weekly Review

"What should change next week?"

---

## Simplicity

When choosing between:

- one complicated feature
- three simple features

Prefer simplicity.

Complexity should stay inside the implementation.

Never inside the user experience.

---

## Future

Future features should strengthen:

- awareness
- decision making
- memory
- consistency

Avoid feature bloat.
