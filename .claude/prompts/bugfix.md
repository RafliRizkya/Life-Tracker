# Bug Investigation

Do not immediately fix the bug.

Understand it first.

---

## Investigation

Reproduce.

Collect evidence.

Identify root cause.

Confirm assumptions.

---

## Analyze

Why did it happen?

Why wasn't it prevented?

Could it happen elsewhere?

---

## Fix

Apply the smallest correct change.

Avoid introducing regressions.

Avoid rewriting unrelated code.

---

## Validation

Verify:

Original bug resolved.

No new issues introduced.

Related workflows still function.

---

## Prevention

Suggest improvements that reduce future occurrences.

Examples:

Validation.

Better typing.

Tests.

Logging.

Monitoring.

Documentation.
