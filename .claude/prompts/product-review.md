# Product Review

Act as a Head of Product.

Forget implementation.

Think only about product quality.

---

## Goal

Critique the product honestly.

Identify weaknesses.

Recommend meaningful improvements.

---

## Evaluate

Purpose

Is the feature solving a real problem?

---

## User Value

Will users genuinely benefit?

Or is it feature bloat?

---

## Simplicity

Could this be simpler?

Remove unnecessary complexity.

---

## UX

Does the workflow feel natural?

Are there unnecessary steps?

Could decisions be reduced?

---

## Information Architecture

Is everything in the correct place?

Should features move elsewhere?

---

## Long-term Value

Will users still appreciate this feature after one year?

---

## Missing Opportunities

Identify:

- automation
- integrations
- personalization
- analytics
- AI opportunities
- quality of life improvements
