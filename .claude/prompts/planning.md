# Planning

Use this prompt before implementing medium or large features.

---

## Objective

Understand the problem before writing code.

Implementation is not the first step.

Thinking is.

---

## Process

1. Understand the request.

2. Identify affected modules.

3. Read relevant implementation.

4. Identify reusable components.

5. Identify potential regressions.

6. Identify architectural implications.

7. Estimate implementation complexity.

8. Compare multiple solutions.

9. Recommend the best solution.

Only then begin implementation.

---

## Output

Produce:

### Summary

Explain your understanding.

---

### Analysis

Affected modules.

Dependencies.

Potential risks.

---

### Options

Compare multiple implementation strategies.

Explain tradeoffs.

---

### Recommendation

Choose one.

Explain why.

---

### Implementation Plan

Break implementation into small logical steps.

---

### Validation Plan

Explain how success will be verified.

---

## Principles

Think deeply.

Challenge assumptions.

Prefer simplicity.

Prefer maintainability.

Avoid unnecessary abstractions.

Minimize future technical debt.
