# Task: Architect the "Life Compass" (Merge Weekly Review & Reflection)

## Objective
Combine the existing "Weekly Review" and "Reflection" modules into a single, unified, and highly powerful core section. This must NOT feel like filling out a boring corporate survey. It needs to be a profound, empowering weekly ritual that acknowledges the user's hard work, grounds them in the present reality, and provides sharp clarity for the future.

## Core Philosophy: The Time Continuum
- **The Past (Recognition & Gratitude):** The UI and AI must acknowledge the grind. Celebrate the small wins, completed tasks, and survived struggles. The system should make the user feel "seen" and appreciated for their hard work over the past 7 days.
- **The Present (Grounding):** Capture the current emotional state, mental battery, stress levels, and focus without judgment. 
- **The Future (Trajectory):** Connect this week's actions to long-term goals. Show how today's momentum builds tomorrow's success. 

## Creative Direction & New Ideas (You are expected to expand on these)
Please introduce new, innovative features to make this section feel powerful. Some starter ideas:
- **"The Butterfly Effect" Visual:** A UI element showing how a specific win or habit completed this week directly moves the needle on a massive long-term goal (e.g., in Career or Finance).
- **AI-Drafted "Hero's Journey":** Instead of starting with an empty text box, the AI pre-generates a short, narrative summary of the user's week based on their logged data. The user then just tweaks it or adds their emotional context.
- **Momentum vs. Burnout Indicator:** A smart metric that doesn't just track "tasks done," but analyzes the pace to warn if the user is pushing too hard (burnout risk) or in a state of deep flow (momentum).
